Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Overview

Bisector App ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information Collection

Bisector App does not collect or transmit any personal information. The app does not require account creation or login.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location services to provide compass headings and save GPS coordinates with your captures. This location data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Fully under your control and can be deleted at any time

You can disable location access at any time through your device's Settings.

Camera Access

The app uses your device's camera to display a live viewfinder so you can sight objects while capturing compass bearings. The camera feed is displayed in real-time only and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Device Sensors

The app uses your device's magnetometer (compass), accelerometer, and gyroscope to calculate headings and device orientation. This sensor data is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.

Data Storage

All capture data (angles, GPS coordinates, timestamps) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to any of your data. You can delete captures at any time within the app.

Network Requests

The app fetches in-app announcements from a public server. This request does not include any personal information or device identifiers. No user data is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Bisector App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store or at info@reliancefiberandpower.com.
