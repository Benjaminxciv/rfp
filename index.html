Features

Precision Bearings

Capture accurate compass bearings using your device's magnetometer with a live camera viewfinder for precise sighting.

Auto Calculations

Automatically calculate bisector angles, reverse bisectors, and line angles between two captured bearings.

GPS Tagging

Save GPS coordinates with each capture. View all your measurements on a map for easy reference.
